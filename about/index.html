About

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University. I was affiliated with the Center for Education and Research in Information Assurance and Security (CERIAS).

I’m currently interested in designing reliable and massively scalable distributed systems. Previously, I’ve worked on multiple areas of information security.
